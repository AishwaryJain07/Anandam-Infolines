Who Needs GST Registration?

  • Businesses operating in India with an aggregate turnover exceeding the threshold limit of 20 lakh.
  • E-commerce operators and individuals supplying goods or services through them.
  • Agents of suppliers and businesses with branches in multiple states or multiple business verticals within a state.
  • Entities previously registered under VAT, Excise Laws, or Service Tax Laws.
  • Individuals providing online information and database access services from outside India to registered taxable persons in India.
